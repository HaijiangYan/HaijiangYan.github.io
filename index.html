About me

Currently a PhD student at Department of Psychology, the University of Warwick.

My research topics could be roughly summarized as "interfacing human cognition and behaviour with machine learning (particularly deep learning) models", which involve several interesting fields including computational modelling in Psychology, Bayesian, computer vision and representation learning. I'm so much into this project, with full of motivation and passion. You can enter the Research section for more details if you wish.

The main purposes I built this website are to (1) develop a corner of thoughts, ideas, practical techniques and literature notes for review and sharing; (2) update current progress of my project; and (3) give myself a sense of rituality in accomplishment of my work.
