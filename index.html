Biography

Hi there! Welcome to the Mr.Yan's Zone!

I was born in Shaanxi, China, where I spent my initial 25 years life with my cute families. I was 1-year delayed to be enrolled in a local kindergarten when I was a child, then I have been always 1-year older than my classmates in all my school life lol.

I was accepted by Peking University (Health Science Center) when I was 19, majoring in Medical Experimental Technology. However, it felt like a boring discipline that I had chosen without any consciousness of personal willings and career plan (a common issue among Chinese high-school students in that age, especially in a developing area, how gut-wrenching it is!), so I studied double degree courses in Psychology there to compensate and fix it, which serves as the beginning moment of my afterward research life, or even the whole career.

Psychology is amazing!
